ABOUT US

We at Lavista Bakery strive for our customers. We bring you the fresh taste of goodness, richness and authenticity.

We have a very vast variety of rich delicious pastries, sponge cakes,hammer cakes, fusion cakes and much more. We have about 10 stores across Mumbai & Thane.

We assure 100 % quality to our customers, and we always provide good, fresh and preservative free products.

Lavista Bakery is one of the fastest growing bakery ventures across Mumbai and we aim to proceed towards all over Maharashtra and ultimately India.

We hope we have brought a smile on your face.
